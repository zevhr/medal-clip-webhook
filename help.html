Help

1 - Why is the thumbnail/timestamp different than mine???
This is either because your clip has the same title as another clip or the caption you entered is not matching the one on the actual clip.
Sadly this is a Medal API limitation that doesn't seem possible to find a workaround for.

2 - Why is the embed not sending???
It could be because it was not able to fetch something from Medal's API, so it didn't send due to an empty field.
If this happens, try another clip. If it still doesn't work, let me know.

3 - What does "offset" mean? (old clip finder)
Offset is how many objects (clips) it will skip before locking on. Set this to a high number (1000 is max) to recieve the first clip you made in that game.

4 - Where do I find my Medal ID?
Your Medal ID is the final set of numbers in the https://medal.tv/users/ URL. Go to your profile on the web and copy those.
For example: I go to https://medal.tv/lulawex and it turns into https://medal.tv/users/215577.

5 - Where do I find a Category ID?
To find a Category ID, head here and press CTRL + F in the left panel, then type the name of your game.
To the left of the game name, look for categoryId and copy that number.
